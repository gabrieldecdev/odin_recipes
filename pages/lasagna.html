Lasagna Recipe


Home

Description

This recipe is for those who love lasagna and want to surprise their guests.
The day is wonderful when there is lasagna.


Ingredients


  • 1 lasagna pasta (ready-made)
  • 500 g ham
  • 500 g mozzarella cheese
  • 500 g ground beef
  • 1 ready-made tomato pasta
  • salt to taste
  • black pepper to taste
  • oregano to taste

Steps


  1. Cook the lasagna noodles in approximately 2 liters of water for 5 minutes.
  2. Cook the ground beef in a pan. Once cooked, add the tomato sauce, salt and seasonings to taste.
  3. Start by assembling a layer of sauce, lasagna noodles, ham and cheese.
  4. Continue this process until everything is finished.
  5. Preheat the oven to 180ยบ C for 5 minutes.
  6. Put the lasagna in the oven for 20 to 30 minutes.
